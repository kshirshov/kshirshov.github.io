Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Find answers to common questions about Quick Note Tab below.

Is Quick Note Tab completely free?

Yes, Quick Note Tab is completely free with no hidden costs, subscriptions, or premium features. Note-taking should be accessible to everyone.

Where are notes stored?

Notes are stored locally on the device for maximum privacy. With Chrome Sync enabled, notes will also sync across Chrome browsers on different devices.

Can I use Quick Note Tab offline?

Yes! Quick Note Tab works completely offline. Notes are stored locally, allowing access and editing even without an internet connection.

Does Quick Note Tab support Markdown?

Currently, Quick Note Tab is designed for plain text notes to maintain simplicity. The focus is on providing a fast, lightweight note-taking experience without formatting complexity.

Can notes be exported?

Notes are stored in Chrome's local storage. Notes can be manually copied and pasted to save them elsewhere. Export features are being considered for future updates.

Is data private and secure?

Absolutely. Quick Note Tab doesn't send notes to any external servers. Everything stays on the device, ensuring complete privacy and security of notes.

Which browsers support Quick Note Tab?

Quick Note Tab is currently available for Chrome and Chromium-based browsers (like Edge, Brave, Opera). The focus is on providing the best experience for Chrome users first.
