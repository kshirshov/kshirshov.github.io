Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Find answers to common questions about Quick Note Tab below.

Is Quick Note Tab completely free?

Yes, Quick Note Tab is completely free with no hidden costs, subscriptions, or premium features. We believe note-taking should be accessible to everyone.

Where are my notes stored?

Your notes are stored locally on your device for maximum privacy. If you have Chrome Sync enabled, your notes will also sync across your Chrome browsers on different devices.

Can I use Quick Note Tab offline?

Yes! Quick Note Tab works completely offline. Your notes are stored locally, so you can access and edit them even without an internet connection.

Does Quick Note Tab support Markdown?

Currently, Quick Note Tab is designed for plain text notes to maintain simplicity. We focus on providing a fast, lightweight note-taking experience without formatting complexity.

Can I export my notes?

Your notes are stored in Chrome's local storage. You can manually copy and paste your notes to save them elsewhere. We're considering adding export features in future updates.

Is my data private and secure?

Absolutely. Quick Note Tab doesn't send your notes to any external servers. Everything stays on your device, ensuring complete privacy and security of your personal notes.

Which browsers support Quick Note Tab?

Quick Note Tab is currently available for Chrome and Chromium-based browsers (like Edge, Brave, Opera). We're focusing on providing the best experience for Chrome users first.
