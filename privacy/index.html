Privacy Policy

Last updated: September 21, 2024

Quick Note Tab is committed to protecting privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information is collected, used, and shared when using the website (quicknotetab.com) and the browser extension (collectively, the "Services").

Information Collection

Information is collected in the following ways:

  • Information provided directly: Information may be collected when contacting via email.
  • Automatically collected information: When using the Services, certain information about the device and usage of the Services is automatically collected through cookies and other technologies. This information may include:
    • Device information (such as hardware model, operating system version, unique device identifiers, and mobile network information)
    • Log information (such as IP address, browser type, pages visited, and other usage data)
    • Information collected through cookies and similar technologies

Use of Information

The collected information is used for various purposes, including to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve the Services
  • Respond to comments, questions, and requests
  • Analyze usage patterns and trends to better understand and improve the Services
  • For marketing and advertising purposes

Google Analytics

Google Analytics is used to collect and process data about the use of the Services. Google Analytics uses cookies to collect information and report website usage statistics. The information generated by the cookie about the use of the website (including IP address) will be transmitted to and stored by Google on servers in the United States. Google will use this information for the purpose of evaluating website usage, compiling reports on website activity, and providing other services relating to website activity and internet usage.

Browser Extension

The browser extension uses Google Analytics to collect usage data. However, it is important to emphasize that the extension does not collect any user information from the notes created. Notes are stored locally in the browser's storage and are not transmitted to any servers or third-party services.

Data Storage

The notes created using the browser extension are stored locally in the browser's storage. For Chrome users, this means notes are saved in Chrome's local storage and are not accessible to any third parties.

In addition to local storage, some information may be stored using cookies on the device. This information is typically used for improving the browsing experience and for analytics purposes.

Furthermore, as mentioned in the Google Analytics section, some data about the usage of the Services is stored on Google Analytics servers. This data is used for analyzing usage patterns and improving the Services. It's important to note that this data is anonymized and does not include the content of notes.

User Choices

Google Analytics can be opted out of by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on. Cookie preferences can be managed through browser settings.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date at the top of this Privacy Policy.

Contact

For questions about this Privacy Policy, contact: qntb@yahht.com
