Privacy Policy

Last updated: September 21, 2024

Quick Note Tab ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information about you when you use our website (quicknotetab.com) and our browser extension (collectively, the "Services").

Information We Collect

We collect information in the following ways:

  • Information you provide to us: We may collect information that you provide directly to us, such as when you contact us via email.
  • Information we collect automatically: When you use our Services, we automatically collect certain information about your device and your usage of the Services through cookies and other technologies. This information may include:
    • Device information (such as your hardware model, operating system version, unique device identifiers, and mobile network information)
    • Log information (such as IP address, browser type, pages visited, and other usage data)
    • Information collected through cookies and similar technologies

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for various purposes, including to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our Services
  • Respond to your comments, questions, and requests
  • Analyze usage patterns and trends to better understand and improve our Services
  • For marketing and advertising purposes

Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics to collect and process data about your use of our Services. Google Analytics uses cookies to collect information and report website usage statistics. The information generated by the cookie about your use of the website (including your IP address) will be transmitted to and stored by Google on servers in the United States. Google will use this information for the purpose of evaluating your use of the website, compiling reports on website activity, and providing other services relating to website activity and internet usage.

Browser Extension

Our browser extension also uses Google Analytics to collect usage data. However, we want to emphasize that the extension does not collect any user information from the notes you create. Your notes are stored locally in your browser's storage and are not transmitted to our servers or any third-party services.

Data Storage

The notes you create using our browser extension are stored locally in your browser's storage. For Chrome users, this means your notes are saved in Chrome's local storage and are not accessible to us or any third parties.

In addition to local storage, some information may be stored using cookies on your device. This information is typically used for improving your browsing experience and for analytics purposes.

Furthermore, as mentioned in the Google Analytics section, some data about your usage of our Services is stored on Google Analytics servers. This data is used for analyzing usage patterns and improving our Services. It's important to note that this data is anonymized and does not include the content of your notes.

Your Choices

You can opt-out of Google Analytics by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on. You can also manage your cookie preferences through your browser settings.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this Privacy Policy.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: mail@yahht.com
